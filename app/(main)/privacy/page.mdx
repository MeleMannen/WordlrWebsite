# Privacy Policy for Wordlr

**Last updated:** June 25, 2026

Wordlr respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the app.

## Advertising

Wordlr displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to serve and measure ads. For more information, please see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Analytics

Wordlr currently uses **Firebase Analytics** to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as app launches and feature usage. This information helps improve the app and identify issues.

## Data Collection

Wordlr does not require you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

## Children's Privacy

Wordlr is intended for a general audience. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
